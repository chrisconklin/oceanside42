Amenities

Enjoy an week at the beach with family and friends.

Living Area

The living room area includes a sofa with pull out queen bed and modern, oversized sitting chairs, a ceiling fan, and a flat screen TV. For our guest's convenience this room is stocked with a selection of board games(Monopoly, Scrabble, playing cards) and books which contain a gamut of topics, from thriller to spiritual. You will definitely find something of interest.

Bedrooms & Bathrooms

Outfitted with three spacious bedrooms.

  • Master bedroom # 1- Bathed in light sea grass green colors the master suite is your own private cabana by the sea. Furnished with a comfy queen size bed, ceiling fan, spacious closet, 5 drawer dresser, large led flat screen TV with cable, and a private full size bath.
  • Bedroom # 2 - This room offers the same warm decor as the master, tucked back from the main living area walls are washed in a warm coral color with soft accents, fitted with a queen size bed, ceiling fan, medium size flat screen led TV, a small dresser and a spacious closet.
  • Bedroom # 3 - The pale warm yellow colors of summer make this room perfect for a summer break, fitted with a set of single bunk beds, a single day bed, a large led flat screen TV with cable, a large dresser with mirror, and a spacious closet.

Kitchen Area

Has a full size refrigerator/freezer, full size oven/stove, microwave, dishwasher, undercabinet radio cd / mp3 player, a table that will seat up to 4 and there are 3 under counter bar stools.
